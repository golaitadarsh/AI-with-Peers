The real talk

Why sit together for this?

AI isn't just for coders or tech folks. It's a tool — like Excel was, like Google was. The people who learn to use it well, early, will have an edge. The people who don't, won't disappear — but they'll work harder for the same results. This session is about getting that head start, together.

🔍

See what's actually possible

Not hype. Not theory. Actual demos of AI doing real tasks — job prep, research, planning, investing — in real time.

🤝

Learn from each other's experiments

Someone in the room is using AI in a way you haven't thought of. Let's surface that.

⚡

Cut your learning curve in half

Instead of each of us fumbling alone, we pool what works. One hour here saves you weeks of trial and error.

⚠️

Know the risks, not just the magic

AI gets things wrong. It hallucinates. It has blind spots. Knowing where humans win is just as important.

The balance

AI is powerful. But it's not everything.

Using AI without understanding its limits is like driving without knowing the brakes. We'll talk about both sides.

🤖

AI is great at

Speed. Pattern recognition. Drafting. Research across 1000 sources in seconds.

🧑

Humans win at

Judgment. Context. Relationships. Knowing when something "feels off."

⚠️

AI risks

Hallucinations. Overconfidence. Privacy leaks. Blind trust without verification.

🛡️

Your edge

Ask better questions. Verify outputs. Use AI as a tool, not a replacement for thinking.
